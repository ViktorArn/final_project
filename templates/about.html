Who We Are

Viktor

They call us a “dynamic duo,” though technically we’re just two guys (one with many delusions), Viktor and Rotkiv, who somehow survived three years of client and uni deadlines, last-minute “just one more tweak” requests, and questionable cafeteria coffee without losing our minds. Viktor is the one you’ll find shimmying up lamp posts for the perfect drone shot.

Meanwhile, Rotkiv has been wage-slaving over pixels for a solid year, though don’t let that fool you into thinking he’s the junior. Armed with an unholy number of Photoshop shortcuts and an obsessive-compulsive relationship with color palettes, he can turn your scribbles on a napkin into a logo that pops so loud it might just break the sound barrier.

Put us together and you get a creative cocktail, served neat, no chaser, with enough caffeine to power a small country.
